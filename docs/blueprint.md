# **App Name**: FortyEight2Freedom

## Core Features:

- Epic Countdown: Displays a large, central timer showing the remaining hours, minutes, and seconds.
- Unit Counters: Shows real-time updates of 'military' time units such as coffee cups, meals, and a humorous 'cigarettes/sleep minutes' counter.
- Mission Progress Bar: A progress bar that fills from 0% to 100% over the 48 hours.
- Stress Relief Buttons: Interactive buttons for comic relief, including a 'Discharge' button with a particle effect of military gear emojis, a 'Hadal' button that distorts the text, and a 'Getaway' long-press button that changes the background to a vacation scene with ocean sounds.
- Excuse Generator Tool: An excuse generator for the last-minute tasks of clearing out, which uses reasoning to pick an appropriate random excuse (like 'I'm on a call' or 'My shoelace broke').
- Soundboard: A soundboard featuring iconic army phrases.
- Grand Finale Effect: When the timer hits zero, triggers confetti of ripped forms, a 'Free!' message, and victory music.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Olive green (#6B8E23), representing the classic IDF color.
- Background color: Desaturated olive green (#D3DABD).
- Accent color: Gold (#FFD700), evoking a sense of celebration and achievement.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk', sans-serif, for a computerized military look.
- Body font: 'Inter', sans-serif, chosen for readability.
- Icons: Military equipment (helmets, duffel bags) with Glassmorphism.
- Subtle hover effects with a green glow on military buttons.
